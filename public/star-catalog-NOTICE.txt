Database v3.8 (https://github.com/astronexus/HYG-Database) by David Nash /
astronexus, licensed CC BY-SA 4.0 (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/).
star-catalog-v1.bin: apparent magnitude ≤ 7.5, rotated to galactic
coordinates, packed as f32 [x,y,z(pc), mag, B−V] per star (little-endian).
star-systems-v1.json: the navigable nearby catalogue, all stars within
25 pc (no magnitude cut), with proper name / catalogue designations
(Gliese/HD/HIP) / spectral type / constellation / distance / position.
